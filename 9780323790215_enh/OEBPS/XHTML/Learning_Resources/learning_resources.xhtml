Case Studies

Welcome to the newly updated Robbins ‘Case Studies Companion. Presented are more than 100 online cases, which use a clinical scenario as a jumping board to discuss the pathophysiology of disease via annotated images, text, and quiz questions. In addition to incorporating contemporary medical knowledge, a strong emphasis has been placed on integration with the online text and other Robbins’ learning resources. Within these cases you will find direct links to relevant sections of the Robbins and Cotran Pathologic Basis of Diseases and Robbins Basic Pathology, figures and tables from these books, Targeted Therapy Boxes, and images. Many cases also contain focused and highly informative short videos on select pathophysiologic topics. These videos can also be accessed directly at the link below. Our hope is that these case studies augment the use of the textbook and stimulate a deeper understanding of the pathophysiology of disease. Over the past 20 years, many colleagues (some former faculty members at UT Southwestern Medical School at Dallas and others at University of Chicago) have contributed to these cases, they are gratefully acknowledged below.
