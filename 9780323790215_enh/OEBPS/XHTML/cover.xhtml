Book cover for Robbins & Kumar Basic Pathology, Eleventh Edition by Kumar, Abbas, Aster, Deyrup, and Das. The design features a vibrant gradient background blending green, purple, and red hues with circular bokeh effects. The title is centered in bold uppercase text, with “ROBBINS & KUMAR” in gold and white, and “BASIC PATHOLOGY” in large white font. Below the title, six circular thumbnail images of histological and pathological sections are arranged in a horizontal row. The authors’ names appear stacked on the lower right. A sticker in the top right corner denotes the inclusion of an enhanced digital version, and the Elsevier logo appears at the bottom left.
