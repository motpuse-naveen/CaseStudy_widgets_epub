Video 10.1 Laboratory Evaluation of Anemia

Laboratory Evaluation of Anemia - describes the initial screening tests in evaluating isolated anemia, compares the role of iron indices in the evaluation of anemia, analyzes the laboratory tests used to evaluate megaloblastic anemia and hemolytic anemias, discusses the value of the direct antiglobulin test (DAT)/Coombs test, and explains the role of gel electrophoresis in diagnosing abnormal hemoglobins.
